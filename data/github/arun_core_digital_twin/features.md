## 💎 Premium Capabilities
ArunCore goes beyond basic chat; it is a full-featured professional proxy system.

### 1. Real-Time Lead Alerts (Telegram)
- **What it does:** The moment a user asks to speak to the "Real Arun" or leaves their contact info, a notification is sent directly to Arun's smartphone.
- **Why it matters:** It bridges the gap between AI interaction and human networking. Arun can see what a recruiter is asking and jump into the conversation on LinkedIn within minutes.

### 2. Identity Grounding (Zero Hallucination)
- **What it does:** The system is explicitly forbidden from "inventing" skills. If Arun hasn't worked with a certain technology (e.g., "Java" or "C#"), the AI will say so.
- **Why it matters:** It preserves the professional integrity of the digital twin. It ensures that every claim made by the AI is 100% accurate to the real-world Arun.

### 3. "Search Efficiency" Logic
- **What it does:** The AI is trained to recognize when it doesn't know something. Instead of looping, it provides a high-quality summary of Arun's *relevant* strengths and offers to flag the question for the real Arun.
- **Why it matters:** This builds trust. Users appreciate an AI that knows its limits and offers a professional "human handoff."

### 4. High-Fidelity Branding
- **What it does:** Integrated official SVG logos for GitHub, LinkedIn, and X with custom hover states and glassmorphism styling.
- **Why it matters:** First impressions are everything. The UI "wows" the user, making the AI twin feel like a premium product rather than a side project.

### 5. Cross-Platform Accessibility
- **What it does:** Optimizes for standard web browsers and mobile devices using `100dvh` viewport management.
- **Why it matters:** Allows Arun to share his link on social media bio headers, knowing it will look great whether opened on an iPhone or a high-res monitor.
